Usability Testing

Usability test was carried out with 5 participants. Participants ranged from 29 to 38 years of age and were all working professionals, a majority of them working in the UI/UX industry.

" The mobile version doesn't feel very responsive... just the same layout shrunken down. "

Overall, participants appreciated the simple and intuitive navigation of the site. The main issues were with finding certain links that weren’t immediately apparent.

  • Difficult to find prototype link

  • Expected to click on image for case study

  • Logo name not easily visible

  • Difficulty finding resume
